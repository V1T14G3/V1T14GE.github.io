Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based and complete basic education where: Students learn in a child-friendly, genders sensitive, safe and motivating environment. Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner Administrators and staff, as stewards of Institution, Ensure an enabling and uppertive environment for effective learning to happen. Family Community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing lifelong learners.

Vision
